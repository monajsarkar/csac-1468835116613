PRE-TRIP Guide

Documents Needed

  • Documents/ Details you should possess before traveling
  • Passport with a Valid Visa Stamped
  • Air Tickets
  • Two extra passport sizes photograph (useful in case of any emergency)
  • Travel Insurance card with coverage & benefits booklet
  • Foreign Exchange (Travelers' Cheque & Cash)
  • Domestic flights require check-in 3 hours prior to departure
  • International airlines recommend check-in 3 hours prior the departure time
  • Organize photocopies of your air ticket, passport & Visa & stock them in your hand baggage / Check-in baggage. Stock all your travel documents in your hand baggage only
  • Customs & baggage: Travelers need to follow rules and regulations declared periodically by the Airport Authorities. Visitors are frisked and usually asked if they have anything to declare on arrival. There are no general restrictions on business accessories like laptops etc. If asked specifically by customs, you can declare it while your exit.
